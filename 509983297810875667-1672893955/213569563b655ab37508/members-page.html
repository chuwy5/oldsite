STATEMENT OF THE BOARD TO THE MEMBERSHIP:
 On July 6TH the Board considered the current circumstances present in the Medford area and determined that government constraints have lifted sufficiently so that the suspension of Club activities announced in April of 2021 are rescinded except that the current Board shall continue until a new Board can be installed.
 However, some extraordinary circumstances continue to exist one of which has the normal Club meeting place closed on the day dictated in the current bylaws.
 It is therefore the Board’s considered opinion that the best possible course of action for the Club and its members is to invoke Article III of the Constitution and authorize Club meetings to be held on any Tuesday through Thursday in the second week of each month.
 The Club will therefore be meeting on Wednesday, July 14th at the New Far East restaurant on the corner of Fourth and Front streets, Medford. The meeting will begin at 6:30pm but the restaurant and the Board recommend arriving earlier as food orders will take longer to process than in the past.
 Club members, guests, and visitors are welcome to attend.
 An agenda will be published on the Club website prior to the meeting and will include nominations for Club Officers as a priority.

 NOVEMBER 8th 2020 ANNOUNCEMENT
CONTINUATION OF CLUB SUSPENSION DUE TO GOVERNMENT RESTRICTIONS
STATEMENT OF THE BOARD TO THE MEMBERSHIP:
On May 8th 2020 the Board considered the extraordinary circumstances present at that time and exercised Article III of the Club Constitution by suspending Club activities and listing exceptions to that suspension. That suspension expires on November 8th 2020 (180 days after implementation).

In light of all known factors, it is the Boards considered opinion that the extraordinary circumstances present on November 8th 2020, while slightly different, remain unchanged in their impact on the Club and that the suspension of Club activities should continue.
To that effect, the Board considered and passed the following:
 
BOARD DECISION:
In light of continuing governmental constraints which prevent normal operation of the Club, the Board declares extraordinary circumstances to exist and hereby invokes Article III of the Club Constitution.
Under the authority of Article III, the Board is implementing the following:
     1) With the following exceptions, ALL CLUB ACTIVITIES ARE SUSPENDED:
          a. Board actions necessary in the best interest of the Club.
          b. Radio nets including the current Monday night Club net.
          c. Maintenance of the Club Website.
     2) Board elections for the 2021 Board shall remain suspended and the 2020 Board, as currently constituted, shall continue to
          oversee Club business until normal operations can resume.
     3) When normal operations are able to resume, Board nominations and elections shall be conducted as a top priority.
     4) All 2020 memberships have been extended for the 2021 year with no payment due.
     5) During this suspension, the Board will confer at least once in each calendar month to evaluate current conditions and
          undertake any necessary Club business.
     6) The May 8th 2020 Board decision to invoke Article III of the Constitution is vacated upon passage of this Board decision.
     7) Per Article III, these actions shall expire not later than 180 days after passage by the Board.
 END OF DECISION
 
In accordance with Article III of the Club Constitution the Board will continue to confer at least monthly to handle any necessary club business and to determine if conditions have changed sufficiently to allow this suspension to be lifted. The results of those determinations will be published on the Club website and announced on the weekly Club Net.
